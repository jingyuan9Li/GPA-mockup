Great Panda Association

That is our job to find out! We chose the adorable great panda as our symbol and brand to emphasize our belief. There is a saying: "When a thing is scarce, it is precious." in an ancient east country, and we have to respect their wisdom: there is nothing more valuable than rare belongings!
